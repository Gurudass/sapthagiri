Our Department

From advanced surgeries to specialized pediatric care, our departments bring together the finest medical professionals and cutting-edge technology to deliver holistic, patient-centered care. Discover the services that make Sapthagiri Hospital a trusted name in healthcare.

General Surgery

Precision in Every Procedure Our General Surgery department combines advanced surgical techniques with personalized care to deliver the best outcomes. From minimally invasive procedures to complex surgeries, our experienced surgeons prioritize patient safety and recovery. State-of-the-art operation theaters and post-surgery care ensure a seamless healing journey.

Obstetrics and Gynecology (OBG)

Comprehensive Women’s Health and Maternity Care Empowering women at every stage of life, our OBG department offers exceptional maternity care, gynecological treatments, and wellness programs. With a focus on safe deliveries and minimally invasive surgeries, we provide a nurturing environment for every woman’s health journey.

Department head: Prof. Jaknil Akia

Orthopaedics

Strengthening Mobility, Restoring Lives Specializing in advanced joint replacements, fracture care, and sports injury management, our Orthopaedics department is committed to restoring your mobility. Equipped with the latest technology and a skilled rehabilitation team, we ensure every patient regains their strength and independence.

Department head: Prof. Jaknil Akia

General Medicine

Expert Care for Everyday Health From routine checkups to managing chronic illnesses, our General Medicine department focuses on accurate diagnosis and holistic treatments. Our physicians work closely with patients to design personalized health plans, ensuring long-term wellness and preventive care.

Department head: Prof. Shikla Brotha

Paediatrics

Compassionate Care for Little Wonders From newborn care to adolescent health, our Paediatrics department offers specialized services to ensure your child’s well-being. With a focus on preventive care, vaccinations, and developmental monitoring, we provide a safe and friendly environment for your child’s growth.

Department head: Prof. Jaknil Akia
