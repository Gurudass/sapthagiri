Pediatric Services We Offer

At Sapthagiri Super Speciality Hospital, we provide a wide array of pediatric services that are designed to address the unique health needs of children at every stage:

  • 1.Normal Delivery & Caesarean Section: Our experienced pediatricians and obstetricians ensure safe deliveries, with specialized neonatal care to ensure the health of your baby right from birth.
  • 2. High-Risk Newborn Screening:For babies born at higher risk, we provide comprehensive screening to detect potential health concerns early, allowing for timely interventions and better outcomes.
  • 3. Vaccination (National Schedule): As part of our commitment to children's health, we offer vaccinations as per the national schedule, protecting your child from serious preventable diseases.
  • 4. Nutrition and Growth Monitoring: Tracking your child’s growth is crucial for ensuring they meet developmental milestones. Our pediatricians provide regular monitoring and expert guidance to ensure proper nutrition and healthy growth.
  • 5. Seizure Disorders: We offer expert diagnosis and management of seizure disorders in children, including epilepsy. Our pediatric neurologists work closely with families to manage and treat these conditions effectively.
  • 6. Neurological Diseases:Our pediatric neurology specialists are experienced in diagnosing and treating neurological conditions affecting children, including developmental disorders, brain conditions, and more.
  • 7. Hormonal Diseases (Thyroid, Diabetes):Pediatric endocrinologists at Sapthagiri Hospital manage hormonal issues such as thyroid disorders and childhood diabetes, ensuring your child’s health is carefully monitored.
  • 8. Adolescent Clinic: Adolescence comes with its own set of challenges. Our adolescent clinic offers specialized care for teenagers, addressing physical, mental, and hormonal changes as they transition into adulthood.
  • 9. General Medicine Services: Our pediatricians treat a wide range of childhood illnesses, including:
    • Fever (Dengue, Malaria, Typhoid)
    • Abdominal pain, diarrhoea, vomiting, and constipation
    • Respiratory problems such as asthma, pneumonia, and more
    • Urinary issues, musculoskeletal pain, and general health concerns
    • Chronic conditions like hypertension, diabetes, and cholesterol management
  • 9. Respiratory Problems: We manage respiratory issues in children, including asthma, bronchitis, and other chronic conditions, ensuring that your child can breathe easier.
  • 9. Minor Pediatric Surgery: Our pediatric team is equipped to handle minor surgical procedures relevant to children. For more complex surgeries, we collaborate with our general surgery department to ensure your child receives the best care possible.
