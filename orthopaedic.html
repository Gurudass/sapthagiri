Best orthopaedic hospital in Hesarghatta, Bangalore

Orthopaedics is a branch of medicine focused on diagnosing, treating, and preventing musculoskeletal disorders, including bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, and tendons. At Sapthagiri super speciality Hospital, we provide the best orthopaedic care in and around Hesarghatta. Whether you're suffering from fractures, arthritis, or complex joint issues, our expert team offers advanced, patient-centered care tailored to your specific needs.

Our hospital is home to some of the top orthopedic surgeons in Hesarghatta and a highly trained medical staff of more than 2700+ members who have successfully treated thousands of patients. We specialize in a wide range of orthopedic conditions, offering services such as fracture repair, joint replacements, spine surgery, and sports injury care. With the latest technology and personalized treatment plans, we ensure that your recovery is as quick and effective as possible.

Orthopaedic Services We Offer

At Sapthagiri super-speciality Hospital, we provide the following orthopaedic services:

  • Fractures and Dislocations: Treatment for both adults and children, including emergency care and long-term recovery strategies.
  • Arthritis: Treatment for primary and secondary arthritis, offering both surgical and non- surgical options.
  • Fungi and Parasitic Infections: Expert care for fungal and parasitic infections of bones and joints.
  • Immunological and Metabolic Disorders: Treatment for immunological disorders like rheumatoid arthritis and metabolic disorders such as rickets.
  • Congenital Disorders of the Skeletal System: Specialized care for birth defects affecting bones and joints.
  • Degenerative Diseases: Advanced treatments for degenerative diseases of the spine, shoulder, hip, knee, and ankle.
  • Bone and Joint Tumors: Expert care for benign and malignant bone and joint tumors.
  • Neglected Trauma Management: Specialized treatments for long-standing injuries and complications.
  • Internal Derangement of Knee and Shoulders: Treatment for joint instability and internal derangement.
  • Deformities of Upper and Lower Limbs: Correction of deformities through both surgical and non-surgical approaches.
  • Osteoporosis: Comprehensive care for osteoporosis and osteopenia.
  • Joint Replacements: Advanced hip, knee, shoulder, and elbow replacement surgeries with excellent post-operative care.
  • Tendon and Ligament Injuries Treatment for sports-related injuries and joint disorders.
  • Amputations and Limb Lengthening Specialized limb lengthening and shortening surgeries, as well as post-amputation care.
