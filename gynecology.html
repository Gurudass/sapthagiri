Our Gynecology Services

We offer a wide range of services for women of all ages, covering everything from general health to specialized treatments:

  • Normal Delivery & Caesarean Section: We provide comprehensive care during and after childbirth, ensuring the health and safety of both mother and baby.
  • Uterus Removal Surgery (Hysterectomy): Minimally invasive procedures to remove all or part of the uterus, providing relief from various gynecological conditions.
  • Cancer Screening Procedures in Women: Early detection of gynecological cancers like cervical, ovarian, and uterine cancer, allowing for more effective treatment.
  • Family Planning Operations: A range of contraception services, including surgical options for long-term birth control.
  • Ovarian Cyst Removal Surgery: Advanced, minimally invasive techniques for the removal of ovarian cysts, promoting faster recovery.
  • Pregnancy and Prenatal Care: Comprehensive management and expert care throughout your pregnancy, from conception to delivery.
  • Postpartum Care: Ongoing support after childbirth, including medical attention for both mother and newborn.
  • Fertility Treatments: Providing support for couples struggling with infertility, including advanced treatments like IVF, IUI, and fertility counselling.
  • Hormonal Therapy: Treatment for hormonal imbalances, including thyroid disorders, diabetes-related issues, and menopause management.
  • Vaginal and Urinary Health: Comprehensive care for issues like urinary incontinence, pelvic pain, and other related disorders.
